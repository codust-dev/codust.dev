# AI for Partial Differential Equations

This module covers a brief traditional numerical methods, Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs), and cutting-edge generative models for PDE solving.

## What You'll Learn

- **Numerical Methods**: Finite difference, finite element, and spectral methods
- **Physics-Informed Neural Networks**: Lagaris method and Raissi's PINNs
- **Generative Models**: Diffusion models for PDE solving
- **State-of-the-Art Techniques**: Latest advances in AI-driven PDE solutions
